Portfolio

JottSpot is a location based social media iOS application I developed based on an idea my brother had while sitting in a lecture during Medical School. Based on a user's location, they can view posts in a half kilometer radius. When they post, they leave that picture behind where they physically were when posting.

Written in Swift, I was the sole developer on the application which will go live on the AppStore in February 2017.
