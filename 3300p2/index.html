NBA Championship Teams' 3's by Year:

This graph shows the nba championhsip teams for the last 35 years. On the y axis you can see each teams' 3 point % that year. You can toggle between 3 point % and 3 point attemtps.
Can you see how the league is changing for 3 point attempts? In addition to the championship teams' 3pt stats, you can add the league average for both catagories as well to see how the champion that year compares to the league. Finally, you can check out the stats for the team by hovering over the team logo on the graph.

Draft Players Effects on Teams' Record

This graph is like a stop action film. First you choose the Draft year you want to see (to find a player you like see the back of the player cards above for their draft year). After you choose a draft year you press the play button to see the effet the player has on 1 year. Hover over each circle to see who it is and some of their stats. Continue pressing play to see the next year and then again for the next etc. If a player helped his team win a title in a specific year, a trophy will appear on that year when they get there in the "stop action film." Notable draft years are Steph Curry: 2009, Dwayne Wade, Lebron James, and Chris Bosh: 2003, and Kobe Bryant 1996.
